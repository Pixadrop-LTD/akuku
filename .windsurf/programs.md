# **AKUKURANUT DEVELOPMENT TRUST (ADT)**

## **THE STRATEGIC PROGRAM PORTFOLIO: 2024–2030**

### **I. PEDATRIC HIV & INTEGRATED CLINICAL SYSTEMS (HEALTH DOMAIN)**

**Strategic Pillar:** *Closing the Gap in Last-Mile Health Delivery*

The ADT Clinical Excellence Program is a high-precision intervention designed to achieve 95-95-95 saturation through the integration of clinical services with community-based psychosocial frameworks.

* **Viral Suppression Optimization:** We leverage "Play Therapy" and Assisted Disclosure models to improve treatment adherence among CALHIV (Children Actively Living with HIV).  
* **The PMTCT Continuum:** Our strategy ensures 100% retention in care for HIV-positive mothers, utilizing the ADT Mobility Network to trace defaulters and facilitate scheduled Viral Load (VL) testing.  
* **Nutritional Rehabilitation:** We deploy WHO-standard nutritional assessments for under-fives, linking clinical findings to immediate agricultural interventions at the household level.

### **II. THE "SCHOOLED" LIFECYCLE ACCELERATOR (SCHOOLED DOMAIN)**

**Strategic Pillar:** *Breaking Intergenerational Poverty Through Educational Equity*

This program mitigates the socio-economic barriers to secondary and tertiary education for Orphans and Vulnerable Children (OVC).

* **Holistic Retention Support:** We address the "cost of schooling" through targeted provision of levies, uniforms, and scholastic materials, while deploying Menstrual Hygiene Management (MHM) kits to prevent school absenteeism among adolescent girls.  
* **Strategic Scholarship Linkages:** ADT acts as a high-impact intermediary, sourcing and securing competitive placements for our scholars in national programs like *Elimu*, *Wings to Fly*, and the *JKF* fund.  
* **Vocational Path-finding:** For older OVCs, we provide high-quality technical training in fashion design and digital literacy, ensuring an immediate transition into the local labor market.

### **III. HOUSEHOLD RESILIENCE & ECONOMIC FORTIFICATION (STABLE DOMAIN)**

**Strategic Pillar:** *Transitioning from Dependency to Market-Driven Self-Reliance*

ADT’s stability framework centers on "Capitalization of the Caregiver." We move beyond subsistence to build diversified economic engines within vulnerable households.

* **The VSLA+ Model:** We manage a network of over 100 Village Savings and Loaning Associations (VSLAs), providing financial literacy and a structured mechanism for micro-credit access.  
* **Productive Asset Injection:** We provide "Launch Kits" (e.g., sewing machines, salon equipment) to caregivers who demonstrate micro-entrepreneurial aptitude, transforming caregivers into business owners.  
* **Agricultural Value Chain Integration:** In partnership with KALRO, we promote climate-smart, bio-fortified crop adoption (Nyota beans/Sorghum), moving smallholder farmers from subsistence to surplus.

### **IV. THE APICULTURE SOCIAL ENTERPRISE (ASL)**

**Strategic Pillar:** *Impact Investing in Sustainable Livelihoods*

The ADT Beekeeping Project is our flagship "Social Enterprise" model. It leverages the local ecosystem to create a high-margin, low-labor income stream for HIV-affected households.

* **Modern Apiary Management:** We are transitioning traditional beekeepers into modern, commercial-scale honey production.  
* **Value Chain Expansion:** ADT is currently seeking partners to scale its honey processing, branding, and distribution capacity, turning a local project into a regional brand that funds the Trust’s social mission.

### **V. THE ADT LOGISTICS & MOBILITY NETWORK**

**Strategic Pillar:** *Scaling Impact through Operational Efficiency*

Social impact is a function of mobility. In partnership with **World Bicycle Relief (WBR)** and the **ADT SACCO**, we have institutionalized a unique mobility solution for our frontline workforce.

* **The Co-Financing Engine:** Our case workers access heavy-duty mobility tools (bicycles/motorbikes) through a sustainable credit model, increasing household visit frequency and ensuring "last-mile" service delivery for remote OVC families.  
* **The ADT SACCO:** A dedicated financial vehicle that provides community members and staff with credit facilities, reinforcing local financial autonomy and organizational sustainability.
