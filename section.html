Our Foundation

Guardian Angels Caring Hands is a Kenyan non-profit organization whose primary work is to rescue destitute children, victims of FGM, teenage pregnancies and general child abuse practices that was founded on the 29th of June 2021.

Located in Nyahururu town, Laikipia county, we offer a great residential space, ensuring each child receives individualized attention and care. We also work closely with other counties like Nakuru, Nyandarua, Kiambu and Nairobi.

Our organization saves lives by advocating against harmful cultural practices and builds futures for the rescued children by giving them a place to call home and access to quality education.

Nyahururu, Kenya Founded June 29, 2021
2021

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded with just 5 children in a small rented house, our journey of compassion began with a simple mission: to provide love and care to those who need it most.

2022

First Permanent Home

Thanks to generous donors, we purchased our first property, providing a stable and loving environment for 15 children with proper facilities and room to grow.

2024

Education Initiative

Launched our comprehensive education program, ensuring all children in our care receive quality schooling, personalized tutoring, and access to essential learning resources.

2025

Expansion & Impact

Expanded our facilities to accommodate 50+ children and launched community outreach programs, extending our impact and creating a ripple effect of positive change.
